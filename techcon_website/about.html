Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most prestigious technology conferences in the world, attracting thousands of participants from across the globe.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing within the technology industry. We aim to inspire individuals and organizations to leverage technology for a better and more connected future.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Known as the first computer programmer, Dr. Ada Lovelace’s groundbreaking ideas on algorithms continue to inspire generations of innovators.

Alan Turing

Alan Turing’s contributions to computer science and artificial intelligence have laid the foundation for modern computing as we know it today.

Grace Hopper

Rear Admiral Grace Hopper pioneered computer programming languages and played a key role in developing COBOL, which is still used today.
